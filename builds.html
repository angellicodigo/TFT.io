Optimal Builds To Run

Knights (Auto Chess and Underlords)

6 knights has always been an viable option to run due to its lack of nerf and changes. They are tanking and a good frontline,which can combine with other low hp support or damaging units. Strong 3 star units, and all around effective item use. Three viable options are 6 knights with:

  1. 4 Glaciers
    • Stack dps items on berseker/troll warlord, can easily constanly force this build.
  2. Three Dragons
    • High magic damage from dragonknight.In auto chess, you can go knights dragon mages with tortolia. In underlords, you can go two dragons,viper or puck, so you can go scaled or another unit.
  3. Two Warlocks
    • The healing makes your frontline last longer, thus your damage is increase. Commonly run with soul reaper/nercophos and desperate doctor/white doctor. Can add one more unit, like a stunner.
